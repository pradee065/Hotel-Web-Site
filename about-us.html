ABOUT US

Originating in the Kingdom of Bahrain in 1990, Isfahani has expanded its footprint to include eleven branches spread throughout the Kingdoms of Bahrain and Eastern Province of Saudi Arabia. This expansion has been a success in keeping pace with the high demand for gourmet Persian cuisine that only Isfahani has been able to provide. The identification of the Isfahani brand and label has been so successful that 5-star restaurants in the region have now included “Isfahani” Kebabs as a principal dish on their menus.

OUR MISSION

To make available to the Bahrain community, residents and visitors, an excellent taste and quality of ancient Persian food and to participate positively in the development of leisure, tourism attractions, and the overall economy of the Kingdom of Bahrain.

OUR VISION

To continue expanding in the Gulf and other regions promoting the name of ISFAHANI from the Kingdom of Bahrain to other parts of the world through direct ownership and franchising.
